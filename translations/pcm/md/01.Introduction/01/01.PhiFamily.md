# Microsoft's Phi family

The Phi models na di most gbege dey do and cost-effective Small Language Models (SLMs) wey dey, e dey pass models of di same size and di next size wey bigger for plenty kain language, reasoning, coding, audio, vision, and math tests. Dis release e increase di choice of better models for customers, e dey give dem better options for make and build generative AI tins.

Di Phi Family start wit Phi-1 for Python Code generation, carry go Phi-1.5 /2 wey base on text and chat completion, Phi-3-mini/small/medium-instruct plus Phi-3.5/4-mini-instruct, plus develop go Phi-3/3.5-vision for vision, Phi-4 wey get strong reasoning, and Phi-3.5-MoE for MoE, now di full-modal model Phi-4-multimodal dey. Wit beta quality data sets, benchumark fit train to compete wit models wey get bigger training parameters.
